Araya is a designer, artist, and researcher working across creative coding, net art, and physical media. With a background in public policy and experience designing within decentralized systems, they translate complex power structures into legible, confrontational, and accessible forms. Their practice interrogates technology's architectures of control, tracing how information asymmetry, surveillance, and visibility gaps determine who gets protected and who gets exposed.

As co-founder and creative director of (WinPrivacy), a creative agency reshaping the culture of privacy, Araya brings warmth, play, and urgency to spaces too often defined by fear. Through digital and physical experience design, WinPrivacy reframes privacy as a feminist and political project. Their work is rooted in the conviction that privacy is where autonomy begins, its ripple effect creating inclusive spaces for self-expression, dissent, and discovery.

Araya has over 5 years of experience in digital product design and front-end workflows, with a strong focus on web3. Specialized in branding, websites, and designing end-to-end apps across the decentralized ecosystem. Skilled in creative direction, UX/UI, design systems, art direction, visual design, and prototyping.

(Go to Dymension website)

Expanded the visual language to extend to UI screens and designed new illustrations for animated motion. The goal was to create an airdrop claiming page that also works as a high-energy marketing experience—introducing users to a new 'dimension' while giving a glimpse of the product’s vibe and potential. The interaction and design had to be bold, splashy, and hype-driven.

Designed an airdrop claiming flow that had to account for two types of users: those from an EVM chain and those from a non-EVM chain, each requiring a different number of steps. The challenge was to streamline the experience while handling errors and filtering out non-eligible claimers without breaking the flow. The design balances clarity with high-energy visuals to keep the experience both seamless and hype-driven.
